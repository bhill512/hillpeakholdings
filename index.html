Buy Box

Pragmatic parameters. Flexible for the right fit.

Core criteria

  • Geography: Colorado (primary), Mountain West (secondary)
  • Deal size: $250k–$1.5M purchase price (flexible for exceptional fits)
  • Cash flow: consistently positive
  • Financials: clean, understandable financials
  • Owner involvement: owner-operated or lightly managed
  • Simplicity: understandable operations; low customer concentration preferred
  • Demand: established customer base with repeat demand

Preferred Business Types

  • ATM routes / cash-access services
  • Local essential services (laundry, vending, light trades)
  • B2B services with recurring revenue
  • Simple service businesses with repeat customers

Examples, not exhaustive.

We favor businesses that are boring in the best way.

Capital & Deal Structures

  • SBA 7(a) and conventional financing
  • Seller financing welcomed on appropriate terms
  • Earn-outs or hybrid structures considered selectively
  • Structures designed to support continuity and seller success

We value alignment over leverage.

Not a fit

  • Heavy CapEx manufacturing
  • Highly regulated healthcare
  • Venture-backed / growth-at-all-costs
  • Businesses requiring daily owner heroics or constant crisis management

How We Transact

Clear steps, respectful process, realistic expectations.

  1. 1

    Intro email + short summary

    Send the basics (industry, location, asking price, cash flow, and any key notes).

  2. 2

    Quick fit check

    We’ll confirm alignment with the buy box and timing before going deeper.

  3. 3

    NDA + CIM review

    Confidential review and thoughtful questions—no time-wasting theatrics.

  4. 4

    Call with broker and/or seller

    Practical discussion around operations, risks, and transition needs.

  5. 5

    LOI outlining structure (including financing mix)

    Clear terms that support continuity and a successful close.

  6. 6

    Diligence and closing

    Seller transition preferred (30–90 days, flexible) to ensure a smooth handoff.
