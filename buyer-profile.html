Buyer Profile

Hill Peak Holdings, LLC

Operator-Led Acquisitions of Durable Small Businesses

Colorado Springs, CO

Overview

Hill Peak Holdings, LLC is an operator-led holding company focused on acquiring and operating cash-flowing small businesses with durable fundamentals. We take a long-term ownership approach, prioritize respectful seller transitions, and favor straightforward operations over complexity.

We are not financial engineers or short-term flippers. We buy businesses we intend to own, operate, and improve responsibly.

Investment Criteria (Buy Box)

Geography

  • Colorado (primary)
  • Mountain West (secondary)

Deal Size

  • Target purchase price: $250,000 – $1.5M
  • Flexible for exceptional fit

Financial Profile

  • Consistently positive cash flow
  • Clean, understandable financials
  • Reasonable customer concentration preferred

Operations

  • Owner-operated or lightly managed
  • Simple, understandable workflows
  • Established customer base with repeat demand

Preferred Business Types (Examples, not exhaustive)

  • ATM routes and cash-access services
  • Local essential services (laundry, vending, light trades)
  • B2B services with recurring revenue
  • Simple service businesses with stable demand

We favor businesses that are boring in the best way.

Capital & Deal Structures

Hill Peak Holdings is flexible and pragmatic in deal structuring when aligned with continuity and seller success.

  • SBA 7(a) and conventional financing
  • Seller financing welcomed on appropriate terms
  • Earn-outs or hybrid structures considered selectively
  • Structures designed to support smooth transitions and long-term stability

We value alignment over leverage.

What We’re Not a Fit For

  • Heavy CapEx manufacturing
  • Highly regulated healthcare businesses
  • Venture-backed or growth-at-all-costs models
  • Businesses requiring daily owner heroics or constant crisis management

Transaction Approach

  1. 1

    Intro email or submission with summary

  2. 2

    Quick fit assessment

  3. 3

    NDA and CIM review

  4. 4

    Call with broker and/or seller

  5. 5

    LOI outlining structure and timeline

  6. 6

    Diligence and closing

    Seller transition preferred: 30–90 days (flexible)

We move deliberately, communicate clearly, and respect everyone’s time.

What Brokers Can Expect

  • Clear buy box and fast feedback
  • Confidential and professional review
  • Realistic diligence and valuation expectations
  • Long-term ownership mindset
  • Direct access to the decision-maker
