Buyer Profile

Hill Peak Holdings, LLC

Operator-led buyer of durable small businesses

Colorado Springs, CO

Overview

Hill Peak Holdings, LLC is an operator-led buyer focused on acquiring and operating cash-flowing small businesses with durable fundamentals—especially in Colorado Springs and El Paso County. We take a long-term ownership approach, prioritize respectful transitions, and favor straightforward operations over complexity.

We are not private equity, not a fund, and not flipping. You’ll work directly with the decision-maker for a fast, confidential review.

Investment Criteria (Buy Box)

Geography

  • El Paso County / Colorado Springs (primary)
  • Colorado Front Range (secondary)
  • Colorado statewide
  • Mountain West (selectively)

Deal Size

  • Target purchase price: $300,000 – $1.0M
  • Target cash flow: roughly ~$250k SDE (flexible for exceptional fit)

Financial Profile

  • Consistently positive cash flow
  • Clean, understandable financials
  • Low customer concentration preferred

Operations

  • Owner-operated is fine; prefer businesses that run without daily owner firefighting
  • Simple, documented workflows
  • Repeat demand and durable local need

Preferred Business Types (Examples, not exhaustive)

  • Essential local service businesses (Colorado Springs / El Paso County)
  • Routes or contract-based services (laundry, vending, ATM, janitorial, maintenance)
  • Commercial maintenance or B2B services with recurring revenue
  • Light trades only if already staffed and systematized (HVAC, plumbing, electrical)
  • Small, niche U.S.-based SaaS only if profitable/breakeven, understandable, low concentration, and not venture-backed

We favor businesses that are boring in the best way: repeat demand, simple operations, and clean books.

Capital & Deal Structures

Hill Peak Holdings is flexible and pragmatic in deal structuring when aligned with continuity and a clean close.

  • SBA 7(a) and conventional financing (SBA acquisition)
  • Seller financing considered on appropriate terms
  • Earn-outs or hybrid structures considered selectively
  • Structures designed to support smooth transitions and long-term stability

We value alignment over leverage.

What We’re Not a Fit For

  • Heavy CapEx manufacturing
  • Highly regulated healthcare / medical practices
  • Highly seasonal or tourism-dependent businesses
  • Venture-backed startups or unproven SaaS
  • Businesses dependent on 24/7 owner availability or daily firefighting
  • High customer concentration or opaque financials

Transaction Approach

  1. 1

    Intro email or submission with summary

  2. 2

    Quick fit assessment

  3. 3

    NDA and CIM review

  4. 4

    Call with broker and/or seller

  5. 5

    LOI outlining structure and timeline

  6. 6

    Diligence and closing

    Seller transition preferred: 30–90 days (flexible), with documentation and training

We move deliberately, communicate clearly, and respect everyone’s time. We also recognize the reality that the new owner may not be on-site full time immediately.

What Brokers Can Expect

  • Clear buy box and fast feedback
  • Confidential, NDA-ready review
  • Realistic diligence and valuation expectations
  • Long-term ownership mindset
  • Direct access to the decision-maker
